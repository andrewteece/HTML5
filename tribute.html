Ansel Adams

Ansel Adams: 1902-1982

  • 1902 - Born in San Francisco on February 20th, only child of Olive and Charles
  • 1906 - Young Ansel is injured during the San Francisco earthquake, breaking his nose and scarring him for life in the process.
  • 1914 - Ansel teaches himself to play piano
  • 1915 - He is removed from mainstream schooling, taught by parents, tutors or himself.
  • 1916 - On a family vacation to Yosemite National Park, California his parents bought him a Kodak Box Brownie camera.
